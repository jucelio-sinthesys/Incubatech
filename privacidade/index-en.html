Privacy and Confidentiality Policy

1. Purpose

This policy ensures the protection, confidentiality, and ethical use of information received by Incubatech during project submission, analysis, incubation, and monitoring.

2. Fundamental Principles

  • Transparency
  • Information security
  • Respect for authorship
  • Informed consent

3. Data Collection

We collect only the data voluntarily provided by applicants through forms and direct interactions.

4. Use of Information

The information is used exclusively for project evaluation, selection, and development purposes.

5. Sharing and Confidentiality

  • No external sharing without explicit authorization
  • Restricted access by authorized staff
  • Secure deletion of data from unselected projects

6. Intellectual Property

Authorship and ownership remain with the applicant. If selected, co-development agreements may be established.

7. Storage and Protection

Data is protected through authentication, encryption, and regular backups.

8. Applicants’ Rights

  • Access, correction, or deletion of data
  • Revocation of consent

9. Updates

Notifications will be issued whenever this policy is updated.

10. Contact

Questions: privacidade@incubatech.org.br

By submitting any proposal, you declare that you have read and accepted this Policy.
