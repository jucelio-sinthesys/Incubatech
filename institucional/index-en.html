Institutional Presentation

Incubatech is an independent virtual incubator that supports the development of startups and scientific-technological projects. We operate in a collaborative network with researchers, entrepreneurs, and public and private institutions.

Our focus is turning ideas into sustainable businesses through strategic mentoring, institutional articulation, and technical support.

Incubatech is part of the H.O.L.O.S. ecosystem and represents one of the pillars of the HALO model (Highly Aligned Lean Organizations). Our approach aims to generate real social and economic impact, with ethics, responsibility, and commitment to innovation.
