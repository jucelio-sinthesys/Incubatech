Institutional

Incubatech is a strategic and fully virtual unit dedicated to identifying, incubating, and accelerating science- and technology-based projects with strong potential for impact and value creation.

We work in collaboration with universities, companies, public institutions, and international networks to turn ideas into viable, scalable, and sustainable ventures.

Our Guiding Principles:

  • Scientific and technical excellence
  • Commitment to concrete and measurable results
  • Ethics, confidentiality, and respect for authorship
  • Collaboration, transparency, and social impact

Although virtual, Incubatech relies on a strong network of partners who can provide physical, technical, and institutional support whenever needed. Our hybrid model ensures agility and outreach without compromising rigor and reliability.

We believe innovation can arise anywhere — and it's our mission to help it go as far as possible.
