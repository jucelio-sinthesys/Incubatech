About Us

Incubatech – Technology Startup Incubator is an independent unit dedicated to the support, incubation, and acceleration of science- and technology-based startups and projects with high business potential.

Our role is to identify innovative ideas, assess their feasibility, support their development, and transform them into scalable, sustainable ventures connected to the future.

We offer strategic mentoring, technical support, institutional articulation, and access to resources so that each incubated project reaches the next level.

Commitment to Results

Incubatech operates with responsibility, professionalism, and market vision. Submitted ideas are evaluated according to technical, organizational, and impact criteria. Those accepted are integrated into a structured incubation and acceleration process.

Transparency and Partnership

The entire process is governed by clear agreements on participation, returns, and intellectual property. Our relationship with incubated projects is based on mutual trust, value generation, and positive impact.
