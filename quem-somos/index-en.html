About Us

Incubatech – Technology Startup Incubator is an independent unit focused on fostering, incubating, and accelerating startups and projects based on science and technology with high business potential.

Our role is to identify innovative ideas, evaluate their feasibility, support their structure, and turn them into scalable, sustainable businesses aligned with the future.

We provide strategic mentoring, technical support, institutional articulation, and access to resources to help each incubated project reach the next level.

Commitment to results

Incubatech operates with responsibility, professionalism, and market vision. Submitted ideas are evaluated based on technical, organizational, and impact criteria. Accepted projects follow a structured process of incubation and acceleration.

Transparency and partnership

All processes are governed by clear agreements regarding participation, return, and intellectual property. Our relationship with incubated teams is based on mutual trust, value generation, and positive impact.

Come build with us

If you have a transformative idea and believe in the power of technology, Incubatech is the right place to take the first step.
