Incubated Project

AI for Automated Interpretation of Public Policies

This project aims to apply Artificial Intelligence to automatically interpret and convert public policy documents into structured, accessible, and actionable information.

The proposal combines techniques such as NLP (Natural Language Processing), semantic analysis, neural networks, and knowledge models to enhance the understanding and implementation of government, regulatory, and administrative guidelines.

The initiative was developed within the L.I.S.I. – Laboratory of Integrated Science & Innovation, and received methodological support from Incubatech for technical validation, business modeling, and institutional articulation.

The project intends to positively impact public administration by promoting greater efficiency, transparency, and civic participation.
